Hineni

Hineni is a you-ship-we-ship focused on helping others with special needs--whether they're an autistic younger sibling, a recovering alcoholic, a wheelchair user, or an elderly grandparent, Hineni provides you the funding and resources to build tools to assist other people. Hebrew for "here I am," the word Hineni represents the act of showing up for other people. This is the essence of this YSWS--showing up and providing support, especially when no one else does. Spend hours on projects, earn points you can spend in shop for both of you.

How It Works

While most YSWS programs are focused on building and shipping something cool, Hineni asks you to build something that truly helps someone else. If you have a friend who's hard of hearing, a cousin who's recovering from addiction, or a wheelchair user, you can pair up with them and spend 10 weeks building tools just for them. If you don't know anyone like that, you can build for Hack Club's partnered autism support groups, recovery centers, or care facilities that desperately need what you can make.
