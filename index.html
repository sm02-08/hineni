Hineni

Hineni is a you-ship-we-ship focused on helping others with special needs--whether they're an autistic younger sibling, a recovering alcoholic, a wheelchair user, or an elderly grandparent, Hineni provides you the funding and resources to build tools to assist other people. Hebrew for "here I am," the word Hineni represents the act of showing up for other people. This is the essence of this YSWS--showing up and providing support, especially when no one else does.
